Our Work

Resin Tables

Our handcrafted resin tables blend natural wood with vibrant epoxy resin to create one-of-a-kind functional art pieces. Each table tells a unique story through its organic patterns and carefully selected materials.

River Tables

Our signature river tables feature stunning blue resin "rivers" flowing through natural wood slabs, creating a breathtaking visual effect that mimics water flowing through a landscape.

Ocean Tables

Inspired by coastal scenes, our ocean tables incorporate multiple layers of colored resin to create depth and movement, perfectly capturing the essence of ocean waves and shorelines.

Geode Tables

These unique pieces mimic natural geode formations using specialized techniques that create crystalline patterns within the resin, often incorporating actual gemstones and minerals.

1

Wood Selection

We carefully source and select premium hardwoods with unique character, grain patterns, and natural features.

2

Design & Planning

Each piece is meticulously designed to highlight the wood's natural beauty while incorporating resin elements.

3

Resin Pouring

Multiple layers of premium epoxy resin are carefully poured and manipulated to create depth and visual interest.

4

Finishing & Polishing

Each piece undergoes extensive sanding, finishing, and polishing to achieve a flawless, glass-like surface.

Micro Cement

Our micro cement applications transform surfaces with a sleek, contemporary finish that combines durability with minimalist beauty. Perfect for walls, floors, and furniture, these finishes create seamless, modern spaces.

Interior Walls

Our micro cement wall finishes create stunning, textured surfaces with a contemporary industrial aesthetic, available in a wide range of custom colors and finishes.

Decorative Flooring

Durable, seamless flooring solutions that combine beauty with functionality, perfect for residential and commercial spaces requiring a modern, low-maintenance finish.

Custom Furniture

From sleek countertops to entire furniture pieces, our micro cement applications create striking monolithic forms with a sophisticated, minimalist appeal.

1

Surface Preparation

Thorough preparation of the substrate ensures perfect adhesion and a flawless final result.

2

Base Application

Multiple thin layers of our specialized micro cement create the foundation for the final finish.

3

Color & Texture

Custom pigments and application techniques create the desired aesthetic, from subtle to bold.

4

Sealing & Protection

High-performance sealants protect the surface while enhancing its natural beauty and ensuring longevity.
